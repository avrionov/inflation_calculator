How This Calculator Works

This tool helps you adjust historic home sales prices for inflation using the Consumer Price Index (CPI) data.

  • Historic Price: The original dollar amount of a sale at a specific point in time.
  • Adjusted Price: What the Historic Price would be worth in today's dollars (as of the latest CPI data available: September 2025).
  • Asking Price: Allows you to compare a modern asking price against the inflation-adjusted value of a historic comparable sale.
  • Over/Under: Shows the difference between the modern Asking Price and the Adjusted Price.
